LEAD SCORING 

Lead scoring is a methodology used to rank prospects against a scale that represents the perceived value each lead represents to the organization. Once leads management process starts, a good database of leads and prospects is created with help of Inbound Marketing campaigns.

This helps to identify at which point of sale these prospects are starting with, assigning numeric values to certain website browsing behaviors, conversion events, or even social media interactions. The resulting score is used to determine which leads should be followed up with directly by a sales rep or which leads need to be nurtured further down the funnel.

Some users might be close to purchasing, while others may need further engagement to make their final decision. It is fundamental to give the correct value to each opportunity for your business.
