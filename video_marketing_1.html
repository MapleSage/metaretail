VIDEO MARKETING FOR YOUR CONVERSION FUNNEL

 

TRANSFORM YOUR LEAD-GENERATION MODEL

We understand, video can produce extraordinary effects when used as part of a marketing campaign. With this in mind, many B2B and B2C companies are still only producing explanatory videos, without exploiting the potential growing every day in video technology. So do you know how video can help you in all parts of the sales funnel?

To get the best ROI, it’s important to have a complete video marketing strategy in place to bring in unknown prospects and guide them along the conversion funnel. We design video marketing strategies that can generate sales through the inbound methodology or through our e-commerce strategies.

VISIBILITY

At MapleSage we know how to squeeze all of the page-ranking juice available from video content to increase your visibility. To do that, we use strategies that will truly generate leads and qualified opportunities for your company.

At MapleSage, we create videos with valuable content to increase awareness of your business amongst your target audience. The way we evaluate and build your brand will improve your position on the market and set you up as a reference in your sector.

 

LEAD GENERATION

Inbound Marketing is based on attracting and converting new contacts, and cultivating new lead for your business. The most important thing to remember here is to get the right information at the right time to gain insights and qualify your leads for sales. 

We create specific video seo, for better lead qualification, better lifespan, with quality content and optimized for generating leads and sales. With our videos, we implement automation techniques to closely follow and profile users who click and watch, by timeline and by number of views to give you the best idea of your possible customers.

 
CONVERSION
 
Once your brand is positioned well, and your are known to the user, once you have delivered and also gleaned information about your customers, then comes the conversion process. Get your best solutions for video seo to these users and get them to the point of making a decision.

LOYALTY

Once the sale is closed, you should start building a network of loyal customers through specific content which delivers your products’ key USPs to the customer. This type of content can work as a vehicle to show to new customers and really deliver the true value of your video seo and your after-sales services.

In most cases, it’s better to upsell than to create new customers. This is ideal for businesses in a mature phase, and can increase ROI from their existing client base.

The key is to build upon users who buy into your brand, we know how to do just that.
